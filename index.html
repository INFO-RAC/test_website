How to embed a map

  • From "Share" functionality in the desired map, copy the "Embed this map" link and put it into the iframe structure
  • The map wil be dispayed (queryable) on your site, whilst it will be managed from the KMaP
  • The sample dataset displayed in this embedded map is the REMPEC accidents database, available on the KMaP
